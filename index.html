About Us

Suravi tours and travels is a Darjeeling based tour and travel agency providing services ranging from the hills of Darjeeling - Kalimpong district to the foothills of Dooars region of West Bengal. We have a great knowledge about the North Bengal tourist destinations and serve the better services to the tourists.

Suravi Homestay

Suravai homestay is a great option for tourists to stay at Mangpoo. uravi Homestay is situated at at an altitude of 3700 ft at Mungpoo village in Kurseong subdivision of the Darjeeling district in the state of West Bengal, India. The homely and commendable accommodations will surely ensure the tourists is nothing short of ‘a home away from home’ experience.
